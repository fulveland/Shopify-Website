Putting on a Wrap Cuff

Loosen the cuff. Slide it onto your wrist.

Pull the tail to tighten.

Squeeze the sides. The slot will pop open.

Slip the tail back through the slot.

Extra tail? Tuck it in!

Pull the tail up...

...around...

....and down!

Life with a Wrap Cuff

Our wrap cuffs are made from the same type of leather as saddles. They take about a week to break in, gradually moulding to the wrist and becoming buttery soft. The bumps around the slits become more pronounced with wear, making them even easier to put on. They are double-sealed with resin, and then massaged with beeswax and oil.
